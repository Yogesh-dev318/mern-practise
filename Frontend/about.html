Identifying Deepfake Characteristics

  • Unnatural Facial Expressions or Movements: Deepfake AI may struggle to perfectly replicate natural facial expressions or movements. Look for inconsistencies such as overly smooth movements, unnatural eye blinking, or strange facial contortions.
  • Mismatched Lip Syncing: In videos, pay attention to lip movements and ensure they synchronize properly with the audio. Deepfakes may exhibit discrepancies between lip movements and speech, indicating potential manipulation.
  • Blurry or Distorted Areas: Deepfake AI algorithms may introduce artifacts or distortions in certain areas of an image or video. Look for blurry patches, pixelation, or other anomalies that suggest digital manipulation.
  • Inconsistent Lighting or Shadows: Deepfake AI may struggle to accurately replicate lighting conditions, resulting in inconsistencies in shadows, highlights, or overall lighting quality within the image or video.
  • Abnormal Backgrounds or Environments: Deepfake AI may generate backgrounds or environments that appear unrealistic or out of place. Look for inconsistencies in scenery, lighting, or spatial relationships between objects within the scene.
  • Unusual Behavior or Actions: Deepfake AI may create images or videos depicting individuals engaging in improbable or physically impossible actions. Use your judgment to assess whether the depicted behavior aligns with reality.
